Bob Russell opened the shop in 1978 cutting keys for the new tract houses going up west of 19th Avenue. Today his daughter Maria runs the front counter and his son Tom drives the truck. We are not a national chain. We are not a call-center forwarding your address to whoever bids lowest. When you call (415) 390-2286 you reach the same shop, the same family, the same neighborhood.

We do one thing — locks — and we do it on the same block we live on. If a job is outside what we handle in-house (electronic access control, large-format safes), we say so on the phone.
